The Computing Society is running a programming competition. The goal
is to write a program to play a virtual version of the simple game,
Connect Four. Microsoft have kindly offered to sponsor us with an XBox
prize, and the writers of the best entries will be considered for a
University-wide team to compete in the upcoming BCS programming
competition. That should be plenty of incentive - so get coding!

More information is available on the CUCS website:

        http://www.cucs.ucam.org/competition.html
        http://www.cucs.ucam.org/

and the deadline for submissions is 28 January
