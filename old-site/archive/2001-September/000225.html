> subroutines can recurse? (Some of my simulations may be of the same

Perl will certainly warn, and possibly blow up after 100 levels of
stack with the same function. Doing O(2^16) sounds incredibly worrying,
and possibly better solved in some other way than recursion, as you'll
need a stack frame for each level. Can you do it with tail-recursion
instead, using intermediate variables rather than stack frames, perhaps
by doing it in Scheme or Lisp?
