Change your tights and or shoes. Had this once at a place I worked, had a
girl that would regularly take out machines due to large static shocks.
Turned out she was generating a huge amount of static electricity.

The other thing to do is to get an anti-static mat put under your desk and
chair, ideally with some form of grounding. That helps to prevent the build
up of static electricity.

Frying your motherboard by static electricity is considered bad form btw :-)
