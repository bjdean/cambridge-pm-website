I think a slightly better way of doing it would be to include something in
your Makefile.PL that generates the module's text in this way, and provides
it as data. This ensures that your module, when linked, is effectively
static, and doesn't require a Net connection, but also provides a nice way
to update it, and an easier way to keep it up-to-date when the format
changes.
