Cam.pm is an adhoc group of Perl programmers in the city of Cambridge (UK). It is not restricted to the University, and anyone can join in. The grouping is mainly to encourage discussion of Perl, however this is not an exclusive topic, and we are often to be found discussing beer and other such distractions.
