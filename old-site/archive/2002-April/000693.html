On Fri, Apr 12, 2002 at 10:49:19AM +0100, Peter Clay wrote:
> 
> What's the most efficient way of determining whether a scalar contains an
> integer or a string?

  ^^^^^^^

probably a regexp. something like /^-?[1-9]\d*\z/

(note \z not $, as $ will let you have a trailing \n, which isn't part of
a number)

But the more question for a number is a classic mess. :-(

And maybe the above should be /^-?[1-9][0-9]*\z/ as Unicode or locales
may mean that \d matches a few other digit characters that maybe you
weren't expecting.
