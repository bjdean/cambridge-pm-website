This is not an entrant, but just to show that I think it's possible to do
something fun in that space, and with those constraints, this is my "entry"
for the scrutiny of the rest of the list. Constraints are that you will
need an ANSI-compliant terminal with at least 32 lines and 50 columns.
There is no constraint on the version of perl needed.

--------8<--------
          /Cam.pm_-_Program_with_Beer/;sub
          r{$d=~s/^(..)//;return(hex$1)};#
         $e                              ="
         02                              0A
        10                                00
        02                                09
        02                                0F
         03                              08
         02                              10
         03                              09
          02                            0F
          04                            0A
          020E040B020D040C020C040D020B020E
          020A020E0C00";$e=~s/\s+//g;1;sub
           g{$l=shift;$d=$e;while(length(
           $d)){($n,$s1,$h,$s2)=(r,r,r,r)
           ;for(1..$n){$s++;$a=(31-$l)>$s
           ?chr(32):"@";print(chr(32)x$s1
            ,"#"x$h,${a}x($s2*2),"#"x$h,
            "\n")}}}print"\e[2J";for$t((
            reverse(2..28))){$s=0;sleep(
            1);print"\e[0;0H";g($t);}$_=
             "Qevax*Orre,*Cebtenz*Crey"
             .",*Pnz.cz\n";s/\*/chr(32)
             /eg;tr/a-zA-Z/n-za-mN-ZA-M
             /;print;m{Cam.pm_-for_info 
              see_our_website_at:.....
