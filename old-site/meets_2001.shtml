The meetings that took place in cam.pm's first year, 2001.

  • Friday 2001-11-16
  • Friday 2001-10-12
  • Wednesday 2001-09-05
  • Friday 2001-08-10

These very brief summaries generally written by Philip Kendall, unless otherwise stated. Any complaints, corrections, etc to me...

Friday 2001-11-16

Not particularly well attended, but that may well be a function of it being announced about 3 days beforehand at the bottom of a thread on something completely different; also not helped by Ian Phillips not being able to recognise us in the Castle. We'll have to look more geeky next time :-)

Followed the usual pattern of food, beer and other substances in the Castle, followed by a trip back to House! for coffee (and toast and mushrooms).

Most notable quote of the evening was from the late arriving MBM: "Oh, you're Becky Corlett!"

Friday 2001-10-12

Another social meet in the Castle; this time we were graced with the presence of both Simon Cozens and some of london.pm. Unfortunately, none of our lot wrote a meet report, so we'll have to make do with one stolen from london.pm and these photos from Paul Mison.

Wednesday 2001-09-05

Our second social meet, again in the Castle. This time no famous Perl personalities, but a brief summary would be that we turned up in the Castle, we drank beer (and related substances), we went to House!, people spodded until Tony stopped people spodding by pulling the ADSL line out of the wall, people went to home/bed (I presume).

Notable moments:

  • "I got run over by a car, but didn't even ladder my tights" (Eleanor Blair)
  • "I like dipping into the onion" (Tony Finch)
  • Tony making a comment about MBM which caused Sue to attempt the nasal insertion of her Guinness.
  • Tabloid newspaper headlines.
  • The summoning of various people by the mentioning of their name (Martin Cooper and Ian Jackson)
  • Ian's three `R's: routing, routing, and something I've forgotten. It was funny at the time, OK?
  • Sue attempting the nasal insertion of her coffee at House!

Friday 2001-08-10

Our very first social meet was graced with the presence of Randal L. Schwartz, author of the Llama book (Learning Perl, O'Reilly Press). Highlights, mostly cribbed from Lucy's report to the list:

  • I think I can safely say the social on Friday night was a success. About 20 people in total turned up - including many non-subscribers who we managed to twist the arms of - and we crammed into the top floor of the Castle between 2 other reserved tables (might try that trick for next time).
  • Lucy talked about her cactus.
  • Randal was safely delivered into our company by my boss's boss (hence my boss's cross-examination of me this morning) at around 9.30. Incidentally, reports from my spies at the Genome Informatics meeting at Hinxton say Randal talked about how perl 6 development has been organised and that they were going to change everything and get it done in 2 years.
  • When MBM let him get a word in edgeways, Randal gave us a slide show from his Stonehenge trip on Thursday, we all signed a random bit of paper (for lack of beer mats) and he promised to come back in November. Possibly. I can't remember the details of the rest due to people buying me beer.
