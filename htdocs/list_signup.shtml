One of the main points of cam.pm is the cam.pm mailing list. The list doesn't have to be about perl, and is often about genetics, probability, beer and other things for which perl can be used.

We've recently changed list server from our own to one on pm.org's servers. The new list can be joined at http://mail.pm.org/mailman/listinfo/cam-pm.

I'll try to get the old membership migrated over if possible.

Or join us on IRC - #cam.pm on irc.perl.org
