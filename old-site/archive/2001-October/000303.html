dorkbot london pre-announcment

  http://music.columbia.edu/cmc/dorkbot/dorkbotlondon/

a new monthly forum for people to show off their works, and chatter.
it's twinned with the year-old dorkbotnyc, which has so far featured
robots, gameboy music, live video processing, talking popcorn, 360
degree virtual theatre, sound sculpture, giant throbbing pillows and
experiments in plant cruelty, and other strange things.  what dorks.

let us know if you'd like to give a 5 or 20 minute talk about
something.

it's still in a planning stage, but we already have a date (nov 7th),
time (7pm GMT) and excellent venue (the boxing club).  please pass the
word on to anyone you think might be interested.

hope to see you there,
