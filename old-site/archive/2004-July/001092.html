Peter,

>> On Tue, 13 Jul 2004 13:59:19, Peter Clay <pete@flatline.org.uk> said:

   > Which versions of Perl are largefile-clean?

All of them since 5.6.0, which was released four-ish years ago.  
Your glibc will need to support O_LARGEFILE.

   > Do I need to do anything special to get largefiles to work?

perl -V | grep USE_LARGE_FILES will tell you whether it's compiled in;
you'd need to compile with -Duselargefiles if it wasn't.  The configure
script always enables it (if the platform supports it) by default.
