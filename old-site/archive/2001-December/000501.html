My loop variables were $a and $b (although in fact, they end up being the 
same variable, as kindly pointed out by PAK). So if you use sort, they
end up being localised, and you can do interesting things with them.

eg @foo=sort {lc($a) cmp lc($b)} @bar; to do a case-insensitive sort.

or, if you want to sort on reverse domain parts, from a list of email
addresses:

@foo=sort {lc(join(".",reverse split/\./,($a=~/^[^@]+@(.*)/)[0])) cmp
           lc(join(".",reverse split/\./,($b=~/^[^@]+@(.*)/)[0]))} @bar;
