>The week in which lectures start is week 0, and full term then runs

See Oxford does it better than that: lectures start on Monday of week 1
and end on Friday of week 8 (no Saturday lectures there!).  Undergrads
must arrive by Thursday 0th week and usually leave Saturday 8th.
Nothing much really happens in 0th week except possibly some college
based exams (collections) and a meeting with your Tutors (Supervisors)
to arrange this term's timetable.
