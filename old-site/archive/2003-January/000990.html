> I should have answered at the beginning. It worries me some that the data
> is stored in a database, rather than in plain text files. Mainly because
> of the content of the repository, and how important it can be.

The dump format's portable, though (well, they've changed it a couple of
times, but there's always a flag branch and a documented procedure for
migration). A trivial script like
http://riva.ucam.org/svn/cjwatson/bin/svn-backup is enough to generate
plain-text backups that you can if necessary edit by hand and reload.

I was worried about this at first too, but it makes sense when
considering how to overcome some of the obvious deficiencies of CVS and
dump/load seems to alleviate robustness problems.
