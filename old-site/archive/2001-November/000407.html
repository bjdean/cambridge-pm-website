> There will be a CUCS talk at 8PM on Wednesday the 14th of November in the
> Hopkinson lecture theatre. Directions follow at the end of this message.
> 
> Merops - a specialist biological database in the post-genome era.
> 
> The exponential growth of biological databases shows no sign of 
> slowing, with many megabytes of new data being added daily.  There is an
> increasing need for sophisticated automatic analysis of this data, to
> extract information that is relevant to biologists and present it in
> useful ways. This talk will present Merops, the protease database, as an
> example of the techniques that can be used in addressing this
> requirement.
> 
> Directions: The Hopkinson lecture theatre is located on the New Museums
> site. A map is available at
> http://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/site-maps/Theatres.html - as the talk starts at 8,
