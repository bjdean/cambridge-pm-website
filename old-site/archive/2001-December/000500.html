He's using $a and $b as the loop variables. When you call sort(), it
calls the subroutine you give it as the comparison method with $a and $b
(in the current package) set to the two items it's comparing. Having now
tested it, it does at least localize $a and $b first, so it won't
clobber the loop unless you do something really inventive, but the name
clash would certainly add to the confusion.
