>So this has to be asked.  What's a continuation?  And why did my last
>message take 4 days to be delivered?

A continuation is pretty much a closure for control flow. If you 
think of each block as having a separate "control" scratchpad that 
notes where it's been, you can capture those along with the variable 
scratchpads. When you invoke a continuation it just puts the control 
frames back at the same time it puts the variable frames back.
